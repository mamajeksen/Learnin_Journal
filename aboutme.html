Hi there! My name is Jess. Welcome to my learning journal.

After a several month of being a super organized Mom of my first born, we decided to have a second baby. This changed a lot, wich I didn´t expect.

Why did I choose to become a mom?

I always knew, that being a mom will be a part of my life. I prepared myself for this, since I was a teenager. After having my first one I didn´t want to have a second one, but yeah, as you might heard, you´ll forget the pain and the struggels of pregnancy and laboring. And also because my son was so easy to handle, I underestimated the dynamic of having two of them. Although I would never undo my decisions. Even if it´s hard and I have to take myself back the most of the time, it is worth it.

Where do I get the power to handle everything?

Honestly? I don´t have it. Without my husband and the Kindergarten I would be lost. For me it works fine to take every help that I can get. And I am learning everyday a lil bit more of being a better mom and wife, while trying not to forget myself through all of it.



Good Friends are very helpfull and also Playdates with other mom´s who know your struggle. And for me coffe and a good breakfast is a secret tip to start the day with good conditions. Finding your personal flow with everything is the key. Not to compare myself and celebrating our achievements and having a good time is what helps me to carry on.
